Methodology

Honest, reproducible, no leakage.

  1. 01
    FetchDaily BTC-USD closes from Yahoo Finance, resampled to a monthly average (~140 monthly observations since 2014-09).
  2. 02
    Hold outLast 12 months are removed from the training set and never seen by the model.
  3. 03
    FitProphet trained on log-prices with yearly seasonality and a flexible changepoint prior.
  4. 04
    ScorePredictions over the held-out window scored on MAE, RMSE, MAPE, and directional hit-rate.
  5. 05
    ProjectRe-fit on the full series, project 6 months forward with an 80% uncertainty band.
