Methodology

Honest, reproducible, no leakage.

  1. 01
    FetchBTC-USD hourly closes from Yahoo Finance, ~17,000 observations over the last 730 days (the yfinance hourly cap).
  2. 02
    Hold outLast 720 hours (30 days) are removed from the training set and never seen by the model.
  3. 03
    FitProphet on log-prices with daily and weekly seasonality and a flexible changepoint prior.
  4. 04
    ScorePredictions over the held-out window scored on MAE, RMSE, MAPE, and directional hit-rate.
  5. 05
    ProjectRe-fit on the full series, project 30 days (720 hours) forward with an 80% uncertainty band.
